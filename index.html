Nursing Sciences Department

Developing compassionate and skilled nursing professionals

Our Nursing Sciences Department offers comprehensive training in general nursing, specialized care, and advanced nursing practices. Students benefit from state-of-the-art simulation labs and extensive clinical rotations at partner hospitals.

Clinical Sciences Department

Excellence in clinical practice and patient care

The Clinical Sciences Department focuses on developing strong clinical skills through hands-on training, case studies, and supervised practical experience in various healthcare settings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about admissions, programs, and student life

What programs do you offer?

We offer various healthcare programs including Nursing, Medical Laboratory Science, Midwifery, Pharmacy Technology, Community Health, and Health Information Management.

What are the admission requirements?

Requirements vary by program but generally include Grade 12 certificate with credits in relevant subjects, entrance exam, and medical fitness certificate.
